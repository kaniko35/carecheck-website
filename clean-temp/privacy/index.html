Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how CareCheck Technologies LLC handles information used with CareCheck Ready and related CareCheck development-stage services.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

CareCheck Technologies LLC ("CareCheck," "we," "us," or "our") is building tools that support childcare operations, classroom organization, and administrative workflows. CareCheck Ready is currently a session-based MVP document generator that helps users create downloadable childcare materials such as labels, classroom signs, and printable documents.

Information You Provide

CareCheck Ready may use information entered by a user, including child name, date of birth, classroom name, optional child photo, roster information, and selected document or label settings. This information is used to generate downloadable childcare materials during the current session.

Session-Based MVP Use

CareCheck Ready is not intended to permanently store child profile information in its current MVP form. Users should download or print the materials they need and should avoid entering unnecessary sensitive information.

Photos and Uploaded Files

Uploaded photos and roster files are used to prepare previews and downloadable materials selected by the user. Users are responsible for ensuring they have permission to use any child photos, roster files, or other information uploaded into the tool.

No Sale of Personal Information

CareCheck does not sell personal information. We may use limited technical information necessary to operate, maintain, troubleshoot, and improve the website and services.

User Review and Responsibility

Users are responsible for reviewing generated materials for accuracy, appropriateness, print alignment, and compliance with their childcare program requirements before use.

Compliance Disclaimer

CareCheck products and services are designed to support childcare operations and administrative workflows. They do not replace licensing requirements, accreditation standards, active supervision, professional judgment, or regulatory compliance obligations.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy may be sent through the CareCheck contact page.
