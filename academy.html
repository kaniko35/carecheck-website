Educational Disclaimer

Use Academy resources as educational support.

CareCheck Academy materials are provided for general educational and organizational purposes only. They are not legal, medical, licensing, or regulatory advice. Programs should review all policies and procedures with qualified professionals and applicable state, local, and licensing authorities before use.

Before adopting a policy

  • Compare it with your licensing rules
  • Review it with leadership and legal counsel when needed
  • Train staff before implementation
  • Keep signed acknowledgements and update records
