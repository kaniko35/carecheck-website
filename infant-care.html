CribCheck

CribCheck is a future safe sleep documentation and reminder tool designed to help childcare staff track scheduled visual sleep checks.

Important: CribCheck is not a baby monitor and does not replace required staff supervision or visual checks.

CribCheck is intended to support NAEYC-aligned safe sleep practices when used outside the crib sleep space.
